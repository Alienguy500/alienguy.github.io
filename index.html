About

Hi I'm Zoë Martin. I'm a programmer with experience in Python, C#, Unreal Engine, and JavaScript, but also in other fields including art, design, and music. I recently started developing my skills further by studying Games Technology at the University of Portsmouth, where I've undertaken a number of modules within my first year that explore the various roles within game development.

As part of my modules I've had the opportunity to explore unfamiliar concepts such as concept art, 3D modelling, and Game Design Documentation, but also to develop my teamworking skills throughout a series of "Game Jams". It was enjoyable to get to work on games as a part of a team and has given me valuable experience in collaborating with other developers, as well as working under time pressure.
